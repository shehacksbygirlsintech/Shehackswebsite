SheHacks

She Hacks is an all-female and non-binary hackathon aimed at bridging the gender gap in the field of technology. We were frustrated with the current gender imbalance and so made it our mission is to inspire and encourage more young women to explore the opportunities in tech, regardless of their programming experience.

Our Background

2019 - was an incredible start for SheHacks - it was amazing to see so many girls turn up and explore their interests in coding. Paired with industry professionals, it was an inspiring and educational event for so many.
​ 2020 , struck with COVID-19, we held a virtual hackathon. Although faced with new challenges, again, as always we were impressed with the quality of work produced during the hackathon. ​
In 2021 , we hosted a successful virtual conference, inviting girls across the region to participate. The event exemplified our commitment to inclusivity, providing a platform for skill development and collaboration in STEM.
Luckily in 2022 , we were able to host an in-person hackathon on UWC campus! Across 2 days, participants got the exciting chance to hear from inspiring speakers, learn new technical skills from mentors and build a project.
Again in 2023 , we were able to host yet another successful hackathon by hosting judges and mentors from top STEM fields. It was a great opportunity for girls with minimal exposure to the field to truly immerse themselves.
In 2024 , we aim to produce an event where girls get to explore their passion for STEM in a workplace environment, that inspires their curiosity and motivates them to create the most successful projects.
